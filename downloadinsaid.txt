How to download INSAID session videos?

Step1: Go to the sessions that you want to download..this case
	   https://programs.insaid.co/courses/take/machine-learning-foundation-12th-May/lessons/7610731-recording-class-12-22nd-june


Step 2: Right click on the videos.Click copy link and thumbail. You will get something like this..

Step 3: From the data you got in step 2..extract the videoid ..in this case wvideo=iwij2tjb4b

Step 4: Open a browser and launch this url  view-source:https://fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/<videoID> In this case the url would be view-source:https://fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/iwij2tjb4b

Step 5. Right click and search ".bin". Get the first search result..in this case..  https://embed-ssl.wistia.com/deliveries/a6cef9d410520c72f873a0a5bcac9be3.bin

Step 6: Put this url into browser..https://embed-ssl.wistia.com/deliveries/a6cef9d410520c72f873a0a5bcac9be3.bin then right click and Save US

Step 7: Within 2 second .bin file will be downloaded.

Step 8: Rename .bin to .mp4
